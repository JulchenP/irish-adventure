The Bernhard Shaw

There’s literally a big blue bus in the backyard of this pub. They serve pizza in there! Need I say more?

Cross Guns Bridge, Drumcondra, Dublin 9, D09 XW44, Irland

photo © https://thebigbluebusireland.wordpress.com/about/

Mongolian Barbeque

Who cares about Irish Gastro Pubs. Sometimes you need something more exotic. At the Mongolian BBQ you can stuff your plate and hand it over to a chef, who will fire It up in front of you. Delicious!

7 Anglesea St, Temple Bar, Dublin 2, D02 K376, Irland

photo © https://thebigbluebusireland.wordpress.com/about/

Han Sung Asian Market

I know it says Asian Market in the title – believe me – this is the best and most authentic asian food you'll ever taste. It's far in the back of the market, just go through.

22 Strand Street Great, North City, Dublin, D01 K8E8, Ireland

The Workman's Club

This is not only a great pub, but it also serves delicious Burgers! On the roof! Don’t miss out on this quirkily decorated greasy rooftop-burger-joint.

10 Wellington Quay, Temple Bar, Dublin, D02 VX36, Irland

photo © https://theworkmansclub.com/spaces/the-rooftop-terrace/

Mama's Revenge Burrito

Another great burrito place! Try it ;)

12 Leinster St S, Dublin 2, D02 H367, Irland

Little Ass Burrito

This is hands down the best burrito in town. Literally of the options are great, I do have fond memories of the chorizo burrito though ;)

Location 1 Temple Bar Music Centre, Curved St, Temple Bar

Location 2 10 Rathgar Rd, Rathmines

Tolteca

Classic fast food Mexican restaurant. Chipotle vibes, Pretty good ;)

21 Suffolk St, Dublin 2, D02 WA48, Irland

Pablo Picante

Don’t go for those fast food places if you can go to independently owned restaurants. Check out Pablo Picante: The interior is quirky and colorful but most importantly, the burrito is freaking goooooood. Next to the restaurant Is the Torta Shop. Here you can get Tortas – traditional Mexican-style gourmet sandwiches.

4 Clarendon Market, Dublin Southside, Dublin, D02 AV84, Irland
